Meet Kyla

Kyla Martin, aka Kyla Ballerina is a 17 year old dancer from Louisville, KY. She first began formal dance traing at 6 years old. Although ballet is her primary style, Kyla trains in Modern, Jazz and Hip Hop as well. ...She trained with Mrs. Morris from 2009 to 2013. In October of 2012, with encouragement from Mrs. Morris, Kyla joined 360 Dance Studio in Louisville, KY in an effort to both intensify and diversify her dance training. There she trained in Ballet, Jazz and Contemporary Dance until May, 2014. During that Summer Kyla attended a Ballet summer intensive, which sparked a desire for more specialized ballet training year-round. As a result Kyla enrolled at the Louisville Ballet School in August of 2014. She continued training at Louisville Ballet School through the June of 18, at which time she opted for training in a more culturally diverse environment; thus she returned to 360 Dance in August, 2018. She continues to train and compete at 360 as a member of the Senior Elite Team 2020-2021 season.
